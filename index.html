What is SpeakMe

Samsung S Voice lets users control their phones by speaking to it. Vlingo gives users the ability to talk to their phones. Siri acts as a human agent inside a phone. These apps look like the limit of voice commands, but they are not. What all these apps do well is give functionality that is provided by the OS, but there is absolutely no way for improvement. You cannot make Siri learn what every app does and understand how to respond to commands related to every app. This is where SpeakMe comes in. SpeakMe leverages Google's amazing vioce recognition tools to provide a platform for software developers to add voice commands to their apps easily. Users install plugins for different apps, and they only speak to SpeakMe to control all of the apps supported by Speak Me. This creates a friendly smartphone environment for blind and visually-impaired people.
